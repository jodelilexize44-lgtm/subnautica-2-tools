Why this site exists

Most launch searches are narrow: Can I run it? When does it unlock? Does co-op work? Where do I start? This site is built around those questions instead of trying to be a full wiki on day one.

Source policy

Hard facts come from official store pages, developer posts, or verified in-game testing. Speculation is labeled. The goal is to stay useful enough for players and clean enough for search engines.
