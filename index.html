Why this site exists

Most player searches are narrow: Can I run it? Is it on Game Pass? Does co-op work? Where do I start? This site is built around those questions instead of trying to be a full wiki overnight.

Source policy

Hard facts come from official store pages, developer posts, or verified in-game testing. Speculation is labeled. The goal is to stay useful enough for players and clean enough for search engines.

Community Data Pipeline

The site should become more than static pages: player screenshots, tests, routes, and discoveries can become verified guide data.
