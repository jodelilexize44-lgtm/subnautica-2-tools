# Subnautica 2 Tools: 48-Hour Content Plan

> Goal: turn the site from a launch shell into a useful, indexable English resource during the first 48 hours after Early Access goes live.

## Operating Rules

1. Do not publish invented item names, recipe costs, biome names, or map locations.
2. Every factual update must come from one of these sources: official store page, developer post, patch note, in-game testing, or clearly labeled community report.
3. Separate "confirmed" from "testing" and "reported by players".
4. Update pages quickly, but keep each page useful. Thin pages should become tools, checklists, tables, or troubleshooting guides.
5. Avoid uploading copyrighted maps or screenshots unless we own them or have permission. Use text tables first.

## Search Console Watchlist

When Search Console starts showing impressions, expand pages in this order:

1. Queries containing "system requirements", "can i run", "settings".
2. Queries containing "co op", "multiplayer", "crossplay".
3. Queries containing "resource", "blueprint", "base".
4. Queries containing "crash", "bug", "fps", "won't launch".
