What To Record

  • Exact blueprint name as shown in-game.
  • Unlock method: scan, story, crafting station, or other trigger.
  • Location hint with spoiler level, not a fake map coordinate.
  • Patch/build date so old unlock routes can be corrected later.

Confidence Labels

  • Confirmed in-game: directly tested in the current build.
  • Official: stated by the developer or store page.
  • Community report: repeated player report, not yet independently tested.
  • Unverified: not ready for guide recommendations.

Do Not Fill Yet

Do not publish tool names, vehicle unlock chains, fragment counts, material costs, or story-gated unlocks from memory, speculation, or old Subnautica assumptions. A blank row with clear evidence requirements is better than a confident wrong row.
