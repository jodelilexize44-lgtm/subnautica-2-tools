Confidence Labels

OfficialStore page, developer post, support article, or official channel.
Direct testObserved in the current build, with platform, date, and method recorded.
External guide reportNamed by a reputable launch-week guide, but still waiting for this site's direct test or screenshot evidence.
Community reportRepeated detailed player reports, still clearly labeled as player-reported.
UnverifiedSingle report, incomplete evidence, or not tested in the current build.
ChangedPatched, contradicted, or replaced by newer information.
BlockedUseful idea, but cannot be published safely without evidence or permission.
