Subnautica 2 Early Access Guide

Subnautica 2 is playable now, but it is still an Early Access / Game Preview game. Use this page to understand what is official, what is still developing, and what to check before starting a long save.

Current Official Status

QuestionAnswer
Playable now?Yes. Early Access began on May 14, 2026.
Finished 1.0?No. It is an active Early Access / Game Preview title.
Expected Early Access lengthUnknown Worlds estimates about 2 to 3 years, while noting that exact timing is hard to predict.
Price later?The Steam Early Access notes say the price will increase after Early Access.

What Is Playable Now

  • Single-player survival on an all-new alien world.
  • Optional online co-op with up to three friends.
  • Several biomes, some narrative content, creatures, and craftables.
  • Base building, tool crafting, scanning, and exploration loops.
  • The Tadpole submersible is named in the official Steam description.

What Is Still Developing

  • More biomes, creatures, craftables, features, and narrative content are planned through updates.
  • Bug fixes and optimization are part of the Early Access plan.
  • More character and customization options are planned through Early Access.
  • Guides about maps, blueprints, and exact resource routes should be checked against the current build.
