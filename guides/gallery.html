Media Rules

  • Do not upload copyrighted music clips unless they are official embeds or the rights are clear.
  • Do not copy player screenshots from public posts without direct permission.
  • Do not use high-spoiler images as thumbnails on beginner-facing pages.
  • Do not present visual bug screenshots as current after a patch unless retested.
