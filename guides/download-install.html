Subnautica 2 Download and Install Guide

Start here if you are deciding where to play, installing for the first time, using Game Pass, or trying to avoid save and co-op trouble before your first long session.

Quick Answer

Early Access is live

Subnautica 2 entered Early Access on May 14, 2026. Official launch messaging points players to Steam, Epic, and Xbox storefronts; Xbox pages list Game Preview support for Xbox Series X|S, PC, cloud, and Game Pass-related access. Always confirm the final buy/install button in your own region and account.
