Mapping Checklist

  • Record biome or area names exactly as shown in-game or official posts.
  • Keep early-game survival routes separate from late-game discoveries.
  • Use depth ranges and landmark descriptions before publishing a visual map.
  • Mark community reports separately until repeated or directly tested.
  • Keep creature sightings out of beginner routes unless they are low-spoiler and useful for safety.

Map Policy

We will not publish a fake interactive map. The first version will use verified text notes, safe-route descriptions, and spoiler labels. A visual map can come later if the data is reliable and legally safe to publish.
