Before You Send Media

  • Send only images you captured yourself or have permission to share.
  • Say whether the site can publish the screenshot and what credit name to use.
  • Mark spoiler level clearly: low, medium, high, or unknown.
  • Do not send copyrighted music files, pirated files, account details, or private player information.
