About Subnautica 2 Tools

A small unofficial fan site built for players who want practical Early Access tools without noise.

What we publish

We publish availability notes, system requirement helpers, co-op setup notes, resource trackers, base planning tools, and update logs. Official facts are sourced. Speculation is labeled.

Editorial policy

Content typeRule
Store and platform factsUse official Steam, Xbox, Game Pass, or developer pages where possible.
Game dataPublish exact resource, blueprint, map, room, and material names only after a source or direct test supports them.
Community reportsLabel them clearly and avoid turning one-off comments into confirmed advice.
Early Access changesUpdate guidance when patches or repeated current-build reports change the answer.

How to read confidence labels

  • Confirmed: official source or direct current-build test.
  • Community report: repeated player reports that still need independent verification.
  • Unverified: useful research note, not ready for a recommendation.
  • Changed: old guidance that needs correction after a patch or better source.

Monetization policy

The site may test display ads, direct sponsorships, or affiliate links later, but monetization should not replace source accuracy. Hardware, Game Pass, controller, or storage recommendations must be disclosed if affiliate links are added. See the advertising and partnership policy for brand safety rules.
