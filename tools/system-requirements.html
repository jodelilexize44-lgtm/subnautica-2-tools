Upgrade Priority

  1. Storage: keep at least 50 GB free, plus extra room for patches and cache files.
  2. RAM: 12 GB is the listed minimum, but 16 GB is the safer Early Access target.
  3. GPU: the listed minimum starts at 6 GB VRAM-class cards; below that is riskier.
  4. CPU: upgrade only after GPU/RAM/storage are no longer the obvious bottleneck.

System Requirements FAQ

Can I run Subnautica 2 with 8 GB RAM?

The listed minimum is 12 GB RAM, so 8 GB is below the public Steam requirement. Wait for real launch benchmarks before assuming it will be playable.

Can I run Subnautica 2 on a GTX 1650?

A GTX 1650 is below the listed GTX 1660 6GB minimum class. Some below-minimum PCs may still launch games, but this page will not recommend buying based on below-spec assumptions.

How much storage does Subnautica 2 need?

Steam lists 50 GB of available space. Keep extra room for Early Access patches and shader or cache files.

Should I trust performance guesses?

Treat them as estimates. Early Access performance can change quickly after patches, so verified current-build reports matter more than speculation.
