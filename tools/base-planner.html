Subnautica 2 Base Planner

A spoiler-light starter base planner for Early Access. Choose the job your base needs to do, then use the location checklist, module priority, co-op roles, and material log before committing resources.

Starter Plan

Use this quick planner as a decision aid. It avoids exact room and material names until they are confirmed in-game, official, or clearly marked as community reports.

Planning Rules

  • Build near a repeatable resource loop, not just a good view.
  • Put storage, crafting, power, and the return route before decoration.
  • For co-op, create labeled drop-off habits before long exploration sessions.
  • Keep one route home visible from the water column or written in the group notes.
  • Move exact room, module, and material names into public tables only after verification.
